Sinopse

As a child, Chihaya Ayase had only one dream: to see her elder sister Chitose become Japan's most successful model. However, upon defending her ostracised classmate Arata Wataya from his bully—Chihaya's childhood friend Taichi Mashima—she discovers the world of competitive karuta and soon becomes enamoured with the sport.

Personagens

Chihaya Ayase The main protagonist of the series, Chihaya is a passionate and determined high school girl with an unwavering love for competitive karuta, a traditional Japanese card game.

Taichi A childhood friend of Chihaya, is a highly intelligent and hardworking student. Initially introduced as a top student lacking interest in karuta, Taichi later develops a deep passion for the game.

Arata Wataya Another childhood friend of Chihaya and Taichi, Arata is a talented karuta player who moves away but returns to the area after several years. He has a deep love for karuta and serves as an inspiration to both Chihaya and Taichi.
