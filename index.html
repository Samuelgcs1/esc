Sinopse

Black Lagoon" is an anime and manga series that follows the adventures of the Lagoon Company, a group of mercenaries operating in the lawless city of Roanapur, Thailand. The story begins with Rokuro "Rock" Okajima, an average Japanese businessman who gets kidnapped by the Lagoon Company while on a business trip. Rock decides to join the group, leaving behind his old life to adapt to the dangerous and violent world of mercenaries.

Personagens

Rock is a central character in the anime and manga series "Black Lagoon." Initially introduced as Rokuro Okajima, a Japanese salaryman, he undergoes a transformative experience after being kidnapped by the Lagoon Company, a band of mercenaries.

Known as "Two Hands," Revy is a Chinese-American woman and a skilled gunman. She's hot-headed, aggressive, and has a brutal approach to conflicts. Revy's exceptional combat skills and proficiency with firearms make her a formidable force within the Lagoon Company.

The leader of the Lagoon Company, Dutch is an African-American ex-military man. He's calm, pragmatic, and serves as a mentor figure to the team. Dutch is known for his leadership skills, tactical expertise, and ability to keep the group together amidst the chaos of their operations.

The tech-savvy member of the Lagoon Company, Benny handles technological aspects and provides support to the team. He's calm, intelligent, and often the voice of reason within the group. While lacking combat skills, his technical knowledge contributes significantly to the team's success.
