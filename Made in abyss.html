Personagens

Riko The protagonist of "Made in Abyss" is Riko, a determined and adventurous young girl who dreams of becoming a first-class Cave Raider, following in the footsteps of her missing mother.

Reg is a humanoid robot that Riko encounters in the Abyss. He possesses incredible strength, agility, and the ability to unleash powerful beams from his hands. Initially, Reg suffers from amnesia and doesn't remember his past or his true identity.
